Settings Phase 2: Quick settings card at the top of Settings holds the two safety knobs you'd revisit most often after onboarding — Strictness (Strict / Trusting / Auto) and Quarantine days. Bound to the same settings field as the canonical rows inside Safety & limits, so editing in either place updates the value; the card is just a one-tap shortcut for calibration.

Parent-row value summaries now show on more categories: Safety & limits shows the current strictness + quarantine in shorthand ("Trusting · 14d"); Appearance shows the current theme when overridden; Data & recovery shows "N deferred" when the deferred-hash queue is non-empty. Connection, Library, and About already surfaced their summaries from Phase 1. The intent is that the root page tells you the state of the system at a glance — non-default values are visible without drilling in.
