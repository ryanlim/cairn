Fix: transient onboarding-screen flash on launch. iOS occasionally returns "item not found" for Keychain reads that should succeed, during the brief post-launch window before the per-app Keychain cache is hot — most commonly after a force-quit + quick relaunch. Bootstrap previously treated a "not found" as authoritative on the first attempt and routed straight to onboarding; force-quitting cleared it on the next launch because the Keychain cache was warm by then. The retry now covers "item not found" for the first 2 attempts (a ~400ms window) before treating it as truly missing. Fresh-install and sign-out flows still route to onboarding correctly, just after the brief retry.
