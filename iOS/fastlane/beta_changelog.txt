Two fixes bundled in this build.

Live Photo phantom quarantine: a user reported 5 phantom "Aging out" entries appearing after running the fast-initial-scan and then re-importing a backed-up cache. All 5 were .MOV files. Diagnosis: Immich processes motion videos at upload time (transcoding/container changes), so the server's stored SHA1 for a Live Photo's paired motion video doesn't match the on-device SHA1. Build 95's attempt to seed both still and motion via livePhotoVideoId imputed wrong-for-phone checksums, which surfaced as phantom deletions once ObservedStore retained the real on-device hash from a prior sync. This build skips Live Photos from imputation entirely — they go through local hashing instead, which gets the right checksums for both halves. Loses the imputation speedup for Live Photos specifically; keeps it for camera-roll stills. Existing phantom entries from build 95–97 need to be manually dismissed via Pending Review.

Plus the timing fixes that were in the build-98 attempt (which failed at the test lane and didn't ship): live ELAPSED clock during initial sync now ticks every second instead of freezing during pre-hash phases; live elapsed labels and activity-feed phase durations now show whole seconds instead of decimals.
