Per-asset hashing detail in the sync sheet. The Hashing phase used to be opaque past the aggregate "N of M hashed" counter — when a sync stalled on a 200MB iCloud video, you had no way to tell whether anything was happening or what was being hashed. The sync detail sheet now surfaces a sub-row under the Hashing card showing the longest-running in-flight asset (filename, total bytes, elapsed once ≥3s) plus a 0–100% download progress bar when the asset is being fetched from iCloud. The "longest-running" pick is intentional — that's the one most likely to be a multi-second iCloud fetch while the other 3 concurrent slots churn through local files in <100ms.
