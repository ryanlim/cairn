New: Settings → Safety rails → "Don't propagate old deletes." When on, cairn silently ignores phone-delete events for photos taken more than N days ago (range 30 to 3650 days; default off). Their server copies stay on Immich; no quarantine entry is written. Use this when you've already curated your Immich library and want to bulk-clean older photos off the phone without those deletions mirroring to the server.

Protects only OLD photos — measured from each photo's capture date (creationDate). Recent deletions still propagate through the normal quarantine path. The Sync activity feed surfaces "Skipped propagation (older than Nd): X" on any sync that filtered deletions, so you can see the cutoff fired without grepping logs.

Also: "Marketing version" in the About sheet is now just "App version."
