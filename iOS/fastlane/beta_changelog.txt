0.4.1 (build 138) — the run history no longer grows without bound.

cairn keeps a local, append-only journal of everything it does. Until now it grew forever and was re-read in full on every sync — fine today, slower over years. This build keeps your most recent ~500 runs in the live history (Status and Runs) and moves older ones into an archive automatically. Nothing is deleted: restore still reaches archived runs, and Export still includes the full history.

You can browse the archive under Settings → Advanced → View archived history. Most testers won't have crossed the threshold yet, so it'll show an empty state until older runs start rotating out — that's expected.

To test: if you have a long history, open Settings → Advanced → View archived history and confirm older runs appear there while recent ones still show on Status/Runs. Restoring a run (including an older one) should still work.
