0.4.1 (build 137) — fixes the spurious sync spinner on reopen.

Reopening the app could briefly spin the sync icon without actually syncing (the status would stay at "1 hr ago" instead of updating). That was a race introduced in the earlier 0.4.1 builds: the catch-up sync and the background-cancel were driven by two separate signals, and on some reopens the cancel landed right after the sync started and killed it. This build reworks that to a single scene-phase observer so the two can't race — the catch-up sync on reopen now runs to completion, and "Last sync details" updates to "just now."

Everything else is the same as build 136 (the auto-sync consistency work + sync timestamps that honor your 12/24-hour setting).

To test: leave the app idle a while (>5 min since the last sync), reopen, and confirm it actually syncs (status updates to "just now") rather than spinning briefly and staying stale.
