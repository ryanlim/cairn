Settings Phase 3: a search bar on the Settings root. Type a keyword and the page replaces its category list with a flat list of matching rows from anywhere in Settings — title first, synonyms (e.g. "icloud," "danger," "rebuild") matched too. Tap a result to drill into the sub-page that owns it.

Closes out the three-phase Settings reshape (hierarchical sub-pages → quick-settings card + summaries → search) from earlier builds. The intent was: frequently-tweaked settings one tap away, set-once knobs grouped behind coherent sub-pages, and a search bar for the "I know it's somewhere" cases.
