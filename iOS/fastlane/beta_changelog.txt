0.4.1 (build 136) — small fix on top of build 135.

The timestamps in Sync detail (the phase timeline and the activity feed) now respect your Settings → time format choice (System / 12-hour / 24-hour) instead of always showing 24-hour.

Everything else is the same as build 135:
- "Last sync details" on Status shows how long ago the last sync ran; each phase-timeline step shows its start time.
- Reopen-after-idle auto-sync is consistent (cold or warm), backgrounding mid-sync cleanly abandons the run, and "Last sync details" always reflects a completed run.
